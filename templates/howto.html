How to use this website?!

This is a "Lost n Found" portal for Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology (VNIT)
Meaning, we can use it to post lost/found items here so as to reach a greater audience!
Greater audience?! We use IFTTT to post automatically to our FaceBook page!
Thus, no one actually has to visit the website to look for new items!

So, if you have lost/found anything recently, go ahead & Submit!
Don't forget to use the IMAGE url field!! Images are catchy & really helpful!
You can browse through Lost or Found items separately.
I have implemented tagging system, so you can also search items by tags!

e.g. MOBILE HB3 etc.

Or try your any other tag by changing the url (F6)

Tags are Case-insensitive, i.e. hb3 & HB3 are equivalent.
