VNIT Lost n Found

This is a web app developed by Ashish Nitin Patil aka !mmorta!.
It's a "Lost n Found" portal for Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology (VNIT)
We use IFTTT to post to our FaceBook page!
Read the Changelog for version 2

The web app is powered by the Google App Engine platform.
It runs on Python.
